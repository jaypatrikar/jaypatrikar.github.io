This class focuses on the kinematics, dynamics and programming of robotic mechanisms. The fundamental techniques apply to robotic arms, mobile robots, active sensor platforms and other computer-controlled kinematic linkages. Arms are the primary application, and we will study 2D and 3D concepts using motors from Hebi Robotics.  [Website](https://sites.google.com/site/robotkinematicscmu/)
